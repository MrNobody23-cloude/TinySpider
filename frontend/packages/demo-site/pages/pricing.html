FAQ

Common Questions

🤔 Can I really use TinySpider for free?

Yes! TinySpider is fully open source under the MIT license. Self-host on your own infrastructure and track unlimited events, forever free with no feature restrictions.

🔒 Is TinySpider GDPR compliant?

By design. We don't use cookies, don't track personal data, and hash all IP addresses with salted SHA-256. Since the data stays on your infrastructure, you maintain full control.

📦 How do I migrate from Google Analytics?

Simply add the TinySpider tracker script to your site and remove the GA tag. You'll start collecting privacy-friendly analytics immediately. No data migration needed.
